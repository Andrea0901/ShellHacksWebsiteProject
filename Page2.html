Having political influence, increase group polarization, while decreasing trust, and a general undermining of civil society are all targeted outcomes of disinformation.

The effects of disinformation has led to people's attitude changing such as leading people to attack telecommunications masts as a response to misleading stories about 5G causing coronvirus.

Mass media has played a vital role in spreading disinformation and hate speech, that has resulted in modern genocides.
