TOOLS TO FIGHT DISINFORMATION

Home   What is Disinformation?   Effects of spreading disinformation   Tools to fight disinformation

BotSlayer: is a program that facilitates in the tracking and detecting potential information that has been manipulated on Twitter.


Captain Fact: is a web-based sets of tools for collaborative online content vertification.


Global Disinformation Index: is a web-based application that assigns a score to news organizations based on their possibility of disinformation on a particular media outlet. This rating system will be a real-time score that will cover all sorts of media.


MediaBugs: is a service for reporting and fixing specific media coverage flaws and problems.


Trive Verify: is a web-based platform and browser plugin that uses blockchain indexing to provide fact-checking using crowdsourcing.
